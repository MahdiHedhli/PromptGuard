That is the strongest possible blog-post claim because it is both reproducible and falsifiable: anyone can clone the repo, run the harness, and confirm or refute it. - **claude CLI compat root-cause is teachable.** "Hardcoded 'index: 0' on rebuild" is the kind of subtle bug that ships when test fixtures don't include extended-thinking shapes. The fix is to mutate original payloads instead of constructing new ones. Lesson for the post: when transforming structured upstream data, prefer mutation over reconstruction so unknown future fields survive.

- **Capture redaction discipline.** The mitmproxy addon redacts Authorization headers to last 4 chars before writing capture files. Even when an operator inspects captures with their own keys in scope, the keys never land on disk. A small detail that reads well in a security-tooling section.

Operators who care about the desktop app see we have looked at it, know what is hard about it, and have a plan. Reads better than "out of scope" without context.
